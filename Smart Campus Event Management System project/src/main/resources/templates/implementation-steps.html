Welcome to Smart Campus

Follow these step-by-step instructions to get started.

1

Explore Dashboard

After proceeding, you will be taken to your personalized dashboard based on your assigned role (Admin or Student).

2

Browse & Manage Events

View upcoming campus events. If you are an Admin, you can create, update, and manage events. Students can browse and search based on specific filters.

3

Register Seamlessly

Find an event that interests you and register instantly. All your registrations are tracked seamlessly under your 'My Registrations' tab.

4

Stay Notified

Leave the portal open and receive automated dashboard alerts and audio notifications when an event you registered for is about to begin.
